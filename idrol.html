Key rules

  • 🥩 Protein: Hit 145–155 g every single day regardless of activity. Spread across all meals (~35–40 g per sitting).
  • 🧗 Climbing days = training days: Climbing is extremely demanding on arms, back, and grip – eat like a training day, including pre- and post-session meals.
  • 🍚 Carb timing: Load carbs around training and climbing. Avoid large carb meals late at night.
  • 📈 Bulk target: Aim for 0.2–0.35 kg per week. Growing too slowly – add 150 kcal on training days. Gaining fat too fast – cut 30 g of carbs first.
  • 💊 Creatine: 5 g daily. The most evidence-backed supplement for strength and muscle growth.
  • 💧 Hydration: 2.5–3.5 litres of water per day. Especially important on climbing days – your fingers and tendons need it.
  • 😴 Sleep: 8–9 hours. Growth hormone peaks during deep sleep – that is when the muscle is actually built.
